Step 1: The Brain Dump Zone

Think about what your ideal follower would be interested in. Brainstorm every possible topic, question, joke, or format you could try. Don't hold back, the best ideas often come from the weirdest places.

Step 2: The Topic Tester Gauntlet

Don't just upload one video on a certain topic and discount it if it flops. You need to post multiple pieces of content surrounding each topic. This is how you gather real data. For example:

If your niche is "Fitness":

  • Video 1: "3 Best Bicep Workouts" (Educational)
  • Video 2: "My Full Day of Eating" (Lifestyle/Inspiration)
  • Video 3: "Gym Fail Compilation" (Humor/Relatable)

Only after testing multiple angles can you decide if the topic has potential.

Step 3: The "What Worked?" Debrief

When a video does well, study it. Become a detective and figure out what made it pop. Ask yourself:

The Hook: What did you say or do in the first 3 seconds to stop the scroll?
The Vibe: Was the editing fast? Was it a chill talking head video? Was there funny text on screen?
The "Secret Sauce": What was the one unique thing, the joke, the surprising fact, the editing trick, that made it stand out?
